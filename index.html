Problem Statement

Design and develop a comprehensive web portal that integrates all services, provides a hierarchical authentication mechanism for users (User, HOD, Principal, Concerned Service Department), and streamlines service management, tracking, and analytics, ensuring efficiency, transparency, and security.

Challenge description with context

Challenge description with context Currently, managing and maintaining various services within an institution, such as electrical, civil works, systems, internet, furniture, and security is a complex and decentralized process. This leads to inefficiencies, delays, and inconsistencies in service delivery, affecting the overall productivity and satisfaction of users. Furthermore, the current system lacks a hierarchical authentication mechanism, leading to unauthorized access and misuse of services.

Users

PSNA Employees(Concern department Admin)

Requirements

  • Request Registration/Approval
  • Hierarchical Authentication and Access Control (User, HoD, Principal, Concerned Service Department - electrical, civil works, systems, internet, furniture)
  • Process Tracking and Monitoring
  • Data Analytics and Reporting
  • End-to-End secured service transmission

Potential Impact

By addressing these challenges and requirements, the web portal aims to create a centralized, efficient, and secure platform for managing and delivering various services within an institution.
